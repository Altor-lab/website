What this comparison is really about

Cognizant is a global IT services and consulting firm. Its AI practice covers enterprise data strategy, cloud migration, AI model deployment at scale, and long-running managed services. These are valuable for large enterprises with multi-year transformation budgets and internal IT teams to operate systems post-delivery.

Altor is structurally different: a focused AI services company where an Altor engineer works inside your stack from day one. The goal is one working production AI system in 14 days — not a strategy program. For Series A-C B2B SaaS companies, this is often the right fit when a specific workflow is costing $80-200K per year in engineering time.
