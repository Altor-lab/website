Altor vs Scale AI for US B2B Enterprises

Scale AI is a data and AI platform built for large annotation programs and model evaluation. Altor is a focused AI services company that deploys a single production AI system — live in your stack in 14 days, not months.

US buyer context: If your team is evaluating AI services vendors for a specific workflow automation (not a broad AI transformation), the scope, timeline, and pricing of these two options are very different.

Altor best for: One focused workflow, live fast

What this comparison is actually about

Most B2B companies evaluating AI services vendors are not choosing between two identical offerings. Scale AI is known for data labeling, RLHF, and model evaluation — services that require large data programs and a long vendor relationship. Altor is a forward-deployed AI services company: we identify one high-cost manual workflow, build the system that automates it, and deploy it in production in 14 days.

The comparison matters because both appear under "AI services company" queries. But the actual buying decision is very different: Scale AI for enterprise data and model programs, Altor for B2B teams that want a specific, accountable AI system running on real data in the shortest possible time.

Choose Altor when…

Your team has one workflow eating engineering time — support investigation, escalation triage, billing diagnosis — and you want it automated in production in under three weeks. You want someone accountable for it working, not a platform your team operates alone.

The 14-day deployment difference

Altor's forward-deployed model means an engineer from Altor is working inside your stack from day one. Week 1: stack audit, system mapping, playbook design. Week 2: read-only integrations live, first AI investigations running on real tickets. Weeks 3-4: playbooks tuned, team trained, system handed over with ongoing accountability.

This is structurally different from a platform engagement where your team must internally manage the AI program. For US B2B SaaS companies that want results before the next board meeting, the speed and accountability difference is significant.

FAQ

Altor. Typical time from kickoff to first live AI system: 14 days. Scale AI engagements are scoped differently and depend heavily on data program size and internal IT process.

What type of company chooses Altor over Scale AI?
