What Altor investigates for Zendesk teams

Common technical ticket types that Altor resolves without engineering escalation:

  • A customer sees a 402 but their Stripe subscription is active — what actually blocked access?
  • Webhook events arrived out of order and downstream state is wrong — was it a retry, a race condition, or a known deploy regression?
  • SSO login stopped working after an OAuth token rotation — config issue, scope mismatch, or provider outage?
  • A customer's usage data looks wrong in their dashboard — stale cache, missing events in ClickHouse, or a billing calculation bug?
  • Feature flag didn't propagate to one customer's tenant — LaunchDarkly config error, or a rollout that excluded their plan tier?

Altor checks the evidence for each of these in the systems that actually contain the answer, then hands your agent a diagnosis they can share with the customer directly.

Why are Zendesk teams evaluating Forethought alternatives in 2026?

Zendesk acquired Forethought on March 11, 2026. Teams not running Zendesk as their primary platform now face roadmap uncertainty — Forethought's development will focus on deep Zendesk integration, which means non-Zendesk workflows may receive less investment over time.

Can Altor work alongside Zendesk AI?
